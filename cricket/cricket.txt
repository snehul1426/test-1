Cricket Bat: Batsmen use a wooden cricket bat to play different shots. The bat has a handle on top and comes in various sizes and materials

Cricket Ball: The cricket ball is made of pure leather and has a spherical shape. In international cricket, red balls are used for test matches, while white balls are used for ODIs and T20s. Pink balls are also used in day/night test matches

Cricket Helmet: Batsmen and wicketkeepers wear helmets to protect their heads from cricket balls. Helmets are made of hard plastic and metal, with a metallic grill on the front to shield the face.

Other Protective Gear: Players also wear additional protective gear, including leg pads, thigh guards, arm guards, elbow guards, chest guards, and gloves.
